## Lore

- `WIDTH_MAP`/`CONTROL_CHARS`/`LIGATURES` are built **at runtime** here
  (`String.fromCodePoint(i).normalize("NFKC")`, range loops, 22-entry ligature literal).
- `UTF8_DETECTOR_RE` is assembled in TS from the generated clue strings (string interpolation
  mirroring Python's `.format()`), with the `u` flag.
- `possible_encoding` patterns are `^...*$`; mirror `test_possible_encoding` (all 256 latin-1
  chars) and add the trailing-newline `"x\n"` case.
- `_build_html_entities` (the `&name;` filter + uppercase variants) lives here, consuming the
  generated html5 dict + the ported `unescape()`.
